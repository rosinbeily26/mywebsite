I kicked off my software development journey in 2022, diving headfirst into web development with a focus on front-end frameworks like Angular. I got my hands dirty on a bunch of healthcare projects, building responsive UI pages from scratch (because who doesn’t love a good challenge?).

As I bounced from one project to another, I eventually found myself dabbling in back-end development. Enter .NET. That’s where I picked up some useful superpowers—C#, LINQ, SQL, and Git. Now I’m practically unstoppable (in my own mind, at least).

Currently, I’m deep into integrating Azure VM services into .NET applications. So, yeah, now I’ve got some cloud and DevOps knowledge under my belt too. If you’re into that kind of thing, let’s talk shop!

These days, my mission is to learn as much tech as humanly possible. I try to keep it exciting—because, let’s face it, tech learning can get a little boring, but I’m doing my best to keep it fun. No snoozing allowed!

When I’m not glued to a screen, I’m probably smashing birdies on the badminton court (I’ve won more tournaments than I’d care to admit). I try to ditch my phone, get moving, and stay active—sometimes running around town hunting for the best new food spots. And, of course, I spend my free time planning trips that, let’s be real, are almost definitely getting canceled.
